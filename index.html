Sliding Blocks: more space, fewer moves

Three 4×4 puzzles, each shuffled with the same 40 random moves, then solved automatically. With the optimal solver, each extra empty space means fewer moves. Switch to the human method and the counts go flat — proof that the savings come from smart solving, not the spaces alone.
